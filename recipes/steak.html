Steak with sweet potatoes and brocolli

The following is a recipe I have used to make a steak dinner. Although you can make steak with any number of different side dishes, in this recipe I have opted for a baked sweet potato, brocolli and capsicum.

Ingredients

  • 1 piece of steak, I normally go for the 200g porterhouse steak available at Aldi
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 0.5kg of sweet potato, diced into 3-5cm long sides
  • 0.2kg of brocolli, chopped
  • 1 large red capsicum, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Various spices, I recommend, cajun and morrocan seasoning

Steps

  1. Turn the oven on to 200 degrees celcius.
  2. Place the sweet potato, brocolli, and capsicum into an over proof dish, and cover with spices and olive oil.
  3. Once oven is at temperature put the vegetables in for approximately 45 minutes.
  4. Chop the onion and cook in olive oil in a skillet on low heat until onion appears glassy.
  5. Crush the garlic cloves and add to the onion. Feel free to add some more spices at this step, I can recommend paprika, italian mixed herbs, cajun spices, moroccan seasoning etc...
  6. Remove the onions and garlic from the frying pan after 5 minutes.
  7. Turn the heat to medium and put the steak in the frying pan. Leave for approximately 3 minutes per side, or until cooked as preferred.
  8. Serve with the baked vegetables and fried onions.
